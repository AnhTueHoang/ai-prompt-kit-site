✉️ Lead Follow-up Prompts

Use AI to write friendly, timely, and personalized follow-ups that convert real estate leads into clients.

← Back to Home

📬 New Inquiry Response

Prompt ideas for responding quickly and professionally when a new buyer or seller reaches out.

  • "Write a warm message welcoming a new lead who asked about a 2-bed condo downtown."
  • "Create a response that outlines next steps and offers to schedule a call."
  • "Summarize key questions I should ask to qualify a new lead from Zillow."

🏡 After a Home Tour

Check in with your client after a showing to gather feedback and keep the momentum going.

  • "Write a follow-up email asking what the client thought of the three homes we saw."
  • "Create a message that gently asks if they’d like to make an offer."
  • "Draft a recap email with links to the listings we toured and next steps."

📆 Long-Term Nurture

Stay top-of-mind with leads who aren’t ready yet by providing value through periodic check-ins.

  • "Write a friendly check-in message for a lead I haven’t heard from in 2 months."
  • "Send a real estate market update for someone planning to buy in 6 months."
  • "Create a quick message linking to new listings that match their earlier preferences."

👻 Unresponsive Leads

Use gentle nudges and value-driven messaging to re-engage cold or quiet leads.

  • "Write a casual text that checks in without sounding pushy."
  • "Send a message asking if they’re still in the market, and offer to help if anything has changed."
  • "Create a final follow-up email with a personal note and call to action."
