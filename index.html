🧠 AI Prompt Kit

Smart prompt ideas to streamline real estate sales, marketing, and customer service.

Project Overview

This project explores how AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude can be used by real estate teams to automate and improve tasks like lead emails, buyer support, and market insights.

Prompt Categories

🏡 Buyer Support

Explain home financing, tour guidance, and answer common buyer questions.

✉️ Lead Follow-ups

Draft personalized follow-up emails, texts, and conversation starters.

📊 Market Analysis

Use AI to summarize trends, property data, and neighborhood insights.

About Me

I'm Jenny Hoang, an Information Systems grad with a passion for AI, data, and making smart tools easier to use. With this project, I aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI and the real estate world.

During my job search, I noticed many real estate teams struggle to integrate AI into everyday workflows. This kit is designed to help agents and teams work smarter — not harder.
