🧠 AI Prompt Kit

Smart prompt ideas to streamline real estate sales, marketing, and customer service.

🌟 Project Overview

This project explores how AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude can be used by real estate teams to automate and improve tasks like lead emails, buyer support, and market insights.

📁 Prompt Categories

  • 🏡 Buyer Support – Explain home financing, tours, and common questions.
  • ✉️ Lead Follow-ups – Draft email responses and touchpoints.
  • 📊 Market Analysis – Summarize trends or insights using AI.
